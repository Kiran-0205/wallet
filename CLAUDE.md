
## Engineering decision log (required)
After any meaningful task — a feature, a fix, an architectural choice, or a
reversed decision — append an entry to docs/DECISIONS.md using the template at
the top of that file. Always record: what was asked, what you did, WHY (the
tradeoff), any decision you changed and why, and any mistake you hit and how
you fixed it. Tag the entry with your agent name and model. Don't skip small
changes. Also commit after each logical unit of work, with a message body that
states what changed and why. On the command /log, write the entry immediately
for work since the last entry, sourcing prompts from docs/devlog/prompts.log
and changes from git.
